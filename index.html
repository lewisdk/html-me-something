About Deirdre

Deirdre was born in a tiny northeast corner of Maine - practically in Canada, but would not have any memories of her birth place, as her family moved overseas to Germany when she was but a few months old. That is where she made her very earliest memories. As fate would have it, though, her family, after living a few other places first, ended up back in Maine, though neither of her parents were either from nor had any family there. It was a great place to grow up, but she couldn't wait to get away and see the world. She joined the Air Force after graduating from college. That's where she met her husband, and neither of them ended up in a job field that would send them overseas. Such luck! They started a family, though and have had many adventures anyway.

Family

Deirdre has 4 children, three girls and a boy, who keep her quite busy. Her husband is still in the Air Force, which has made establishing a career or pursuing education a bit tricky. As a family, they love to go camping, on bike rides and hikes, playing games (her middle daughter in particular is big into games), and cook and eat food. Her older daughters danced in the Nutcracker ballet this past Christmas season, and are currently very busy with girl scout activities. All three girls dance, and her son is not yet old enough to do extra curriculars - she is sure outright chaos will be life once he is also old enough.

Work and Hobbies

Deirdre has attempted to pursue a professional degree a few times, only to be thwarted by either the addition of a child to their family or a move - yay military life! (Don't misread that - she loves the opportunity to experience new places.) Since the birth of her oldest, she delved deeper into a lifelong passion of hers: photography. She had an adorable little model to practice on, so why not? She has maintained a photography business over the past 8 years, starting up again every time they have moved (which has been about 3 or 4 times). It's challenging, but the work makes her happy and gives her a creative outlet, if not a steady income. When she saw the opportunity to learn coding through launchcode, though, she jumped at the chance! Perhaps, just perhaps, she could learn coding well enough to find a job as a front end web developer. She had been looking into online schools to get into the field, but the programs she had been finding were, as her husband put it, full of fluff. She would love to be able to develop a beautiful website for her own business - none of the templates she finds ever end up fitting her vision - as well, as perhaps make a living designing them for others.

Photography

As mentioned, Deirdre is a photographer. When she sits and thinks about what she wants to do in her life, the answer is and always has been, if she's being honest, photography. Previous to having children, she thought she would try to be a photographer for National Geographic; travel the world and get paid to take photos of what she saw. I mean, who doesn't want to get paid to do what they love? They say you never work a day, if you are doing what you love, right? She didn't end up doing that, obviously, but started a family, which led to photographing babies, children, and families instead. It's wonderful, and she loves it, but does still hope to be able to travel for photography and perhaps put together a book someday. Perhaps, coding will help her to be able to accomplish this. Deirdre currently specializes in maternity, newborn, child, and family photography. She does occasionally photograph boudoir and weddings, and welcomes it as a change of pace!
